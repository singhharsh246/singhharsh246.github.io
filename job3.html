Senior Data Analyst at Venture Hive

Remote

June 2020 - July 2021 (part-time)

As a senior data analyst at Venture Hive, a startup accelerator, I was responsible for creating and managing data pipelines, automating the process of data collection, and setting up the data analytics department. This involved working closely with cross-functional teams to understand their data requirements and designing solutions to meet those needs.

One of my primary responsibilities was to develop and maintain robust data pipelines that could efficiently collect, process, and store large volumes of data from multiple sources. I utilized cutting-edge technologies such as Python, SQL, and cloud-based platforms like GCP to build these pipelines, which allowed us to automate the entire data collection process, saving time and resources.

In addition to building data pipelines, I also set up the data analytics department at Venture Hive. This involved creating data models, developing algorithms to extract insights from data, and visualizing the results in a meaningful way. This helped us to identify trends and patterns, make data-driven decisions, and optimize our operations.

As a part of my job, I had the opportunity to interact with entrepreneurs from around the world who were part of Venture Hive's accelerator program. This allowed me to learn from their unique challenges and perspectives and apply those learnings to improve our data analytics processes.

Overall, my experience as a senior data analyst at Venture Hive was both challenging and rewarding. I was able to use my technical skills to automate and streamline data collection and analysis, while also gaining insights from the startup community that helped me to improve our processes and add value to our operations.
